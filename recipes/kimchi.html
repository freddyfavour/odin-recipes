Ingredients

  • 1 napa cabbage
  • 1/2 cup sea salt
  • 1 tbsp grated ginger
  • 1 tbsp minced garlic
  • 1 tbsp sugar
  • 3 tbsp fish sauce
  • 2 tbsp soy sauce
  • 2 tbsp Korean red pepper flakes (gochugaru)
  • 4 green onions, chopped
  • 1 carrot, julienned

Steps

  1. Cut the cabbage into quarters, rub salt, and let it sit for 2 hours.
  2. Rinse the cabbage, drain, and squeeze out excess water.
  3. Mix ginger, garlic, sugar, fish sauce, soy sauce, and red pepper flakes to create a paste.
  4. Coat each cabbage leaf with the paste.
  5. Sprinkle green onions and julienned carrots over the cabbage.
  6. Roll each cabbage quarter tightly and pack it into a jar or airtight container.
  7. Seal the jar and let it ferment at room temperature for 1-5 days.
  8. Refrigerate the Kimchi once fermented.
