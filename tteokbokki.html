Chicken Tteokbokki

Descriptions

An exquisite encounter between chicken and tteokbokki. This menu became a hot topic when it appeared at the TV show Alley Restaurant in Korea. A menu at the border between Spicy chicken soup and Tteokbokki. I was curious about the taste, so I made it. Then shall we make it together?

Ingredients

  • 300g of lean chicken leg
  • 1 green onion
  • 1/2 onion
  • 2 kelp
  • 1/8 cabbage
  • Tteokbokki rice cake 300g
  • 3 fish cakes
  • 5 sesame leaves
  • 2 tablespoons of red pepper paste
  • 3 tablespoons of red pepper powder
  • 2 tablespoons soy sauce
  • 1 tablespoon chopped garlic
  • 1/2 tablespoon oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon spicy curry powder
  • 1/2 tablespoon pepper
  • 3 tablespoons oligosaccharide
  • a little salt
  • 600ml water

Steps

Step 1
Cut green onion, cabbage, onion and sesame leaves. Roll the sesame leaves and slice them thinly.

Step 2
Cut the fish cake and chicken leg into bite-sized pieces and season the chicken leg with a little pepper and salt.

Step 3
Add cooking oil and grill the chicken thighs. Add 2 tablespoons of red pepper paste and fry in oil first. Then, stir-fry with the chicken thighs.

Step 4 Add 3 tablespoons of red pepper powder, 2 tablespoons of soy sauce, 1 tablespoon of minced garlic, 1/2 tablespoon of oyster sauce, 1 tablespoon of spicy curry powder and 600ml of water and boil. Put 2 kelp in the middle and boil.

Step 5 Add the sliced onion, cabbage and green onions. Then, add tteokbokki rice cake and fish cake and boil. Add 3 tablespoons of oligosaccharide and some black pepper and mix well. Put the chicken tteokbokki in a bowl and then add the sliced sesame leaves to make a delicious chicken tteokbokki.
